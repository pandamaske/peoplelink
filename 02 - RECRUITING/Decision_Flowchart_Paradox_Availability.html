Configuration Note

Paradox availability is configured at the country/legal entity level in Oracle Recruiting Cloud. When creating a requisition, the system automatically determines which features are available based on the requisition's country assignment. Contact your local HR team or HRIS administrator for the current list of Paradox-enabled countries.
