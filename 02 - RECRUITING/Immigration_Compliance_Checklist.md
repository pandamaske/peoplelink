## 1. Overview

This document provides a comprehensive checklist for GEODIS recruiters, hiring managers, and HR teams when hiring foreign national candidates who require immigration sponsorship. It covers PERM (Program Electronic Review Management) labor certification, visa sponsorship requirements, and Department of Labor (DOL) compliance.

### When This Checklist Applies

- Candidate requires H-1B, L-1, or other work visa sponsorship
- Candidate is on OPT/CPT seeking employment sponsorship
- Position may be filled by green card sponsorship candidate
- Candidate requires PERM labor certification process
- Any recruitment involving foreign national work authorization

## 4. Candidate Evaluation - Compliance Requirements

### 4.1 U.S. Worker Testing (PERM)

| Requirement | Description | Status |
|-------------|-------------|--------|
| Good Faith Recruitment | Genuine effort to recruit U.S. workers | [ ] |
| Consistent Evaluation | Apply same standards to all candidates | [ ] |
| Lawful Rejections Only | Reject only for job-related reasons | [ ] |
| No Tailoring | Don't tailor requirements to sponsored candidate | [ ] |
| Document Everything | Record all hiring decisions with rationale | [ ] |

### 4.2 Prohibited Practices

**Do NOT:**
- Tailor job requirements to match the foreign national's qualifications
- Reject U.S. workers for reasons not related to job requirements
- Use different evaluation criteria for sponsored vs. non-sponsored candidates
- Post job internally only to comply with requirements
- Fail to interview qualified U.S. applicants
- Use job requirements that exceed actual business need

   |

   |
   v (30 days minimum + 30 days quiet period)

   |

## 11. Compliance Checklist Summary

### Before Posting Job:
- [ ] Confirm GEODIS sponsorship policy approval
- [ ] Request Prevailing Wage Determination (if PERM)
- [ ] Document genuine minimum requirements
- [ ] Obtain immigration counsel guidance

### During Recruitment:
- [ ] Complete all required advertisements
- [ ] Document all applicants and dispositions
- [ ] Apply consistent evaluation standards
- [ ] Maintain compliant interview records

### At Offer Stage:
- [ ] Verify salary meets prevailing wage
- [ ] Include appropriate contingency language
- [ ] Coordinate with immigration counsel on timing
- [ ] Document offer approval chain

### Post-Hire:
- [ ] Establish Public Access File
- [ ] Set up compliance monitoring
- [ ] Schedule PERM filing if applicable
- [ ] Brief employee on obligations
